# Appalachian Trail

James and I hiked the Appalachian trail SOBO (Southbound) from Khatadin, ME to Rockfish Gap, VA. We were planning to go all the way to Georgia, but unfortunately, we had to stop due to Hurricane Helene.

We created a photo blog for the adventure at [picturesofaforest.com](https://picturesofaforest.com)
