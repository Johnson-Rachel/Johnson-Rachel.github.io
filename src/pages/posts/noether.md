# Amalie Emmy Noether and Noether's Theorem

Submitted as final project for Math 20180. 

## Abstract

This paper presents an introduction to the life of Amalie Emmy Noether (1882-1935) and Noether's Theorem. The early life and education of Noether is examined as well as Noether's gradual acceptance into the mathematics community as an outstanding woman in her field. Finally, Noether's Theorem on the relationship between symmetries and conservation laws is discussed.
