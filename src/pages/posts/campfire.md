# Campfire

Campfire is a web app that brings people together and eliminates food waste through a system of food cameras that allow users to easily share leftover food. 

Won 3rd Place at the Hesburgh Library Hackathon at Notre Dame with Gus Hauge and Jonathan Pal.

The project is on [GitHub](https://github.com/Johnson-Rachel/Campfire).
