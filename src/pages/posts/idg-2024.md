# Irish Dancesport Gala 2024

The Notre Dame/Saint Mary's Ballroom Dance Club hosts an annual ballroom dance competition called the Irish Dancesport Gala. This year, as an officer I had the task of not only competing in my last ballroom dance competition but also helping to run the competition.
