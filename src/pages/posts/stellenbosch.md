# Study Abroad in Stellenbosch

I studied abroad in Stellenbosch, South Africa during Feb-June 2023. I learned so much about the culture, geography, history, and ecology of South Africa and had lots of incredible experiences! 

I bungee jumped from the highest commercial bungee jump in the world.
<img src="/src/assets/bungee.jpg" alt="bloukrans bungee" width="80%"/>

I hiked some cool mountains and waterfalls.
<img src="/src/assets/jonkershoek-1.jpg" alt="waterfall at Jonkershoek" width="80%"/>

I also danced sokkie with the Afrikaaners and learned to be patient with lots of load shedding!
